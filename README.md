# Samsung Supply Chain & Logistics Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
This project delivers an end-to-end Supply Chain & Logistics Analytics Dashboard to monitor and analyze key operational areas, including supplier performance, inventory management, shipment efficiency, and customer insights.

The solution centralizes critical KPIs into a single interactive dashboard, enabling faster and more informed decision-making across the supply chain.

---

## Objectives
- Improve overall supply chain efficiency and visibility  
- Reduce operational and procurement costs  
- Identify high-performing and underperforming suppliers  
- Optimize inventory levels and reduce stock-related risks  
- Monitor and minimize shipment delays  
- Support data-driven sales and customer strategies  

---

## Dashboard Components

### Overview
Provides a consolidated view of core business metrics:
- Revenue, profit, and profit margin  
- Order quantity and shipment volume  
- High-level operational performance indicators  

**Impact:**  
Enables stakeholders to quickly assess business performance and identify trends or anomalies.

---

### Supplier Analysis
Evaluates supplier performance using key metrics:
- Lead time  
- Quality score  
- Unit cost  
- Order distribution  

**Impact:**  
Supports supplier benchmarking and helps identify cost-saving and efficiency opportunities.

---

### Inventory & Production
Analyzes inventory health and production performance:
- Inventory value and safety stock levels  
- Inventory turnover rate  
- Product defect rate  
- Production cost trends  

**Impact:**  
Improves inventory planning, reduces overstock/stockout risks, and highlights quality issues.

---

### Shipment Analysis
Monitors logistics and delivery performance:
- Shipment volume  
- Delay frequency and patterns  
- Carrier performance  

**Impact:**  
Enhances distribution efficiency and helps reduce delivery delays.

---

### Customer Analysis
Provides insights into customer and sales behavior:
- Sales distribution across platforms  
- Demand trends  
- Revenue contribution  

**Impact:**  
Supports better sales strategy and channel optimization.

---

## Tools and Technologies
- Power BI (Data Visualization)  
- Data Modeling and Transformation  
- Business Intelligence and KPI Design  

---

## Key Outcomes
- Centralized KPI monitoring improved visibility across supply chain operations  
- Identified inefficiencies in supplier lead time and cost structure  
- Highlighted inventory risks and product quality issues  
- Supported faster, data-driven operational decisions  

---

## Usage
1. Open the Power BI (.pbix) file  
2. Navigate through each dashboard page  
3. Use filters and visuals to explore insights and trends
